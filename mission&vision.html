Our Mission

To promote, develop, and implement sustainable tourism initiatives that benefit local communities and showcase the unique beauty of Botswana to the world.

Our Vision

To be the leading tourism organisation in Africa, offering unforgettable and authentic travel experiences in Botswana.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote Botswana as a premier destination for tourists by highlighting our rich culture, wildlife, and natural beauty. We strive to provide exceptional travel experiences that contribute to the sustainable development of the tourism industry while preserving the environment and empowering local communities.

Botswana Tourism is dedicated to fostering a welcoming, safe, and enriching environment for visitors, and we aim to increase awareness of Botswana's unique heritage and world-class attractions to a global audience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to position Botswana as a leading tourism destination globally, recognized for its remarkable natural resources, commitment to conservation, and immersive cultural experiences. We aspire to ensure that Botswana remains a sustainable and desirable destination for future generations while driving economic growth through responsible tourism practices.

We envision a future where tourism plays a central role in preserving Botswana's ecological integrity, supporting local communities, and driving sustainable economic development for all.

Our Core Values

  • Conservation: We are committed to protecting Botswana's natural and cultural heritage through responsible tourism practices.
  • Sustainability: Our approach promotes long-term environmental, social, and economic sustainability for all tourism stakeholders.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and new ideas to enhance the tourism experience in Botswana.
  • Collaboration: We believe in working together with local communities, stakeholders, and partners to achieve common goals.
  • Integrity: We are dedicated to ethical practices, transparency, and accountability in all that we do.
