About Botswana Tourism

Discover who we are and what we do to promote tourism in Botswana.

Who We Are

Botswana Tourism Organisation is a national body dedicated to promoting Botswana as a premier tourist destination. Established with the aim of increasing tourism's contribution to the country's economy, BTO works to preserve our cultural heritage and protect natural attractions while developing sustainable tourism initiatives.

Our Mission and Vision

With increased tourism in Botswana, The Botswana Tourism Organisation has a great mission it intends to fulfill and has its eyes set on seeing the accomplishment of its vision.
